the work.

I treat every system, process, and platform as a problem to be understood before it can be solved — working backward from outcomes to architecture, not the other way around.

My background spans healthcare IT at ChristianaCare Health System, enterprise software at Boeing, and start-ups through The Clay Collective. Across all three, the throughline is the same: translating what stakeholders need into requirements that technical teams can act on, then seeing it through to adoption.

As a continual learner and technologist, I'm increasingly focused on how AI tooling changes the way analysts do their work. I don't just read about these shifts — I've built workflows around them.
